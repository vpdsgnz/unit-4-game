Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your score total is more than the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you, so click on it to figure it out.

Each time when the game starts, you will be given a new random number and the values of each crystal will change.
